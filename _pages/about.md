Welcome to my academic homepage!


I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at <a href="https://www.dlut.edu.cn/">Dalian University of Technology</a> and an Assistant Professor at the <a href="https://www.impan.pl/">Institute of Mathematics, Polish Academy of Sciences</a>. At Dalian University of Technology, I work with Prof. <a href="https://faculty.dlut.edu.cn/liuzhenxin/">Zhenxin Liu</a>, and at the Polish Academy of Sciences, I work with Prof. <a href="https://www.impan.pl/~gutman/">Yonatan Gutman</a>. I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from the <a href="https://en.ustc.edu.cn/">University of Science and Technology of China</a> in 2024 under the supervision of Prof. <a href="https://faculty.ustc.edu.cn/huangwen1/zh_CN/index/994143/list/index.htm">Wen Huang</a>. During my doctoral studies, I was a Visiting Ph.D. Student at <a href="https://www.durham.ac.uk/">Durham University</a> from March 2023 to March 2024, where I worked with Prof. <a href="https://www.durham.ac.uk/staff/huaizhong-zhao/">Huaizhong Zhao</a> and Prof. <a href="https://www.durham.ac.uk/staff/chunrong-feng/">Chunrong Feng</a>. 


My research interests lie primarily in dynamical systems and ergodic theory. I am also interested in the interactions of dynamical methods with probability theory, operator algebras, and other related areas. More recently, I have been exploring possible applications of dynamical ideas to geometry.


My work has been published in journals such as *Advances in Mathematics*, *Annals of Applied Probability*, *Ergodic Theory and Dynamical Systems*, and the *Journal of Differential Equations*.
