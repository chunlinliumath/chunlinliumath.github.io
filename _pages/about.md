Welcome to my academic homepage!

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Dalian University of Technology and an Assistant Professor at the Institute of Mathematics, Polish Academy of Sciences. I obtained my Ph.D. in mathematics from the University of Science and Technology of China, and during my doctoral studies I had the opportunity to visit Durham University.

My research interests are mainly in topological dynamics and ergodic theory. I am also interested in related topics in probability theory and C*-algebras.


My work has been published in journals such as *Advances in Mathematics*, *Annals of Applied Probability*, *Ergodic Theory and Dynamical Systems*, and the *Journal of Differential Equations*.
